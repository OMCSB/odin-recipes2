Salmon Yakitori

Description

A simple yet delicious grilled salmon

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup light soy sauce
  • 1/4 Cup sake
  • 1/4 Cup mirin
  • 2 Tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 Salmon fillets
  • 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil

Step-by-step Driections

  1. Mix the soy sauce, sake, mirin, and sugar in a shallow dish; place the salmon fillets in the dish, skin side up. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over medium-high hotness. Channel the salmon and hold the marinade. Sear the salmon 4 to 5 minutes for every side, bit by bit adding the marinade to the dish, this will increase the flavor and help to keep the salmon wet. Serve once the salmon is cooked through and is effectively chipped with a fork.
